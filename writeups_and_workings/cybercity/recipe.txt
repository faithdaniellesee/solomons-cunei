Bake it like a Chef.


Ingredients.
80 g sugar
117 eggs
114 g lard
112 g garlic
102 cups oil
108 yeast
84 ml water
118 ml milk
97 g salt
110 g dough
121 g cream
101 honey
98 g butter
88 g parsley
103 g cheese

Method.
Put cheese into the mixing bowl. Put butter into the mixing bowl. Put salt into the mixing bowl. Put parsley into the mixing bowl. Put garlic into the mixing bowl. Put milk into the mixing bowl. Put cream into the mixing bowl. Put honey into the mixing bowl. Put dough into the mixing bowl. Put water into the mixing bowl. Put yeast into the mixing bowl. Put oil into the mixing bowl. Put lard into the mixing bowl. Put lard into the mixing bowl. Put eggs into the mixing bowl. Put sugar into the mixing bowl. Liquefy contents of the mixing bowl. Pour contents of the mixing bowl into the baking dish.

Serves 1.
